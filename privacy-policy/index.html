GymTrack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

GymTrack respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the GymTrack mobile application.

Information We Collect

The application may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, phone number, or email address
  • User login credentials
  • Gym membership information
  • Attendance data such as Punch In / Punch Out
  • Device information (device model, OS version, app usage)

How We Use Information

Collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing trainer and member accounts
  • Tracking gym attendance
  • Providing gym management services
  • Improving application performance
  • Maintaining application security

Location Information

GymTrack may collect location information when users perform attendance actions such as Punch In or Punch Out. This information is used only for attendance verification and is not shared with third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All communication between the application and the server is secured using encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Sharing of Information

GymTrack does not sell, trade, or rent user personal information. Information may only be shared with authorized gym administrators or when required by law.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services such as Google Play Services that may collect information used to improve application functionality. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

GymTrack does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Retention

User data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide gym management services and fulfill legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account by contacting the support email below.
