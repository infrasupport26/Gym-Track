Privacy Policy for GymTrack

Effective Date: 17-03-2026

GymTrack ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GymTrack mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

By using GymTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Age or date of birth
  • Gender
  • Profile information

1.2 Health and Fitness Data

GymTrack collects and processes the following health and fitness data:
  • Body measurements – including height, weight, body fat percentage, BMI, and other physical metrics
  • Workout data – exercise types, sets, reps, weights, duration, and intensity
  • Workout plans – trainer-assigned or user-created exercise programs
  • Diet and nutrition data – meal plans, calorie intake, macronutrient information assigned by trainers
  • Fitness assessments – results of fitness tests and evaluations conducted by trainers
  • Progress data – historical fitness records, performance trends, and milestone tracking
  • Photos and videos – progress images or videos uploaded voluntarily by users for fitness tracking
  • Attendance records – gym check-in and session attendance history

How We Use Your Health and Fitness Data: This data is used solely to provide the GymTrack service, including enabling trainers to create personalised workout and diet plans, track member progress, conduct fitness assessments, and manage gym memberships. We do not use health data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

1.3 Usage Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage statistics
  • Log information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Connect trainers and gym members
  • Assign and track workout and diet plans
  • Monitor and display fitness progress
  • Manage gym memberships and attendance
  • Conduct and store fitness assessments
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send notifications related to workouts, plans, and updates

3. Media Access (Photos and Videos)

Users may voluntarily upload photos or videos to track fitness progress. These files are stored securely and are accessible only to the user and their assigned trainer within the platform. We do not use these images or videos for any purpose other than fitness progress tracking.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal or health information. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within the platform – between gym members and their assigned trainers
  • Service providers – trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating the app (e.g., cloud hosting, push notifications), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal compliance – when required by applicable law or legal process
  • Safety – to protect the safety of users or the security of the platform

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal and health data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

7. User Rights

  • Access your personal and health data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Children's Privacy

GymTrack is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal or health data from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, and push notifications. These providers have access only to the data required to perform their specific functions and are prohibited from using it for other purposes.
